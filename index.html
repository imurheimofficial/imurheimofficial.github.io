About URHEIM

Pop • Rock • Ethnic • Electronic • Dance

Creates music across multiple genres, with a strong love for pop at the core, while often exploring rock influences, EDM energy, rap elements, and stripped-back arrangements built around guitar or piano. Deeply inspired by Arabic, Egyptian, and other ethnic instruments, he blends these influences into a broader musical identity. URHEIM represents a multi-genre artistic vision where emotion, atmosphere, rhythm, and cultural texture come together in a distinctive and constantly evolving sound.
